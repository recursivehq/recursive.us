Why Recursive?

Do what you love.

We'll handle the rest.

You're a freelancer. You like doing good work. You like being able to set your own rates with clients you like. You don't like having to start your own LLC and manage it. You don't like having to calculate quarterly taxes. You definitely don't like handling individual health insurance. Signing up with Recursive lets you keep all the good parts of the freelancer life and removes the frustrations.

How it Works

Sign up.

When you join Recursive, you'll sign on as an hourly W-2 employee. You do your freelance work with your rate and your clients. We'll handle working with accountants and we can also help negotiate your rates and contracts!
